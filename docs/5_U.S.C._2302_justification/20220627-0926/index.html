Press letters, main binder

  •   Press letters 2021-22.pdf

This PDF binder includes the email-body of the letters with the context (in most cases email #2 of 2 or #3 of 3, and the text of previous emails in their chain) which I sent national and regional outlets from August 2021 through September 2022, as forwarded to their final DHS and FEMA recipients. The email attachments, including the text of emails 0/1/2 that I sent to recipients before I figured out a workable way to include that information in the same chain, are below.

I assembled this binder in an attempt to compactly communicate to subsequent recipients the history relevant to the referral to FEMA's internal investigators (OPR referral September 2022.pdf in folder Referral context) which brought an abrupt halt to what I had expected would be the final stop on my dismally unsuccessful nine year whistleblowing career. That (the assembly, redaction, and filesize-reduction of the binder above) took me so long that when the non-disclosure agreement (NDA) requirement came seemingly out of the blue (in reality I had misjudged the timing of the process, but because I'd done so it caught me by surprise) in December I still wasn't done yet.

That new NDA requirement -- together with my experience of how the combination of the last NDAs I signed, and management's continuation of the problems that I had reported but could no longer talk about, effectively closed off any further attempts to resolve them internally (Detail (full).pdf page 4 top) -- is why I have to finish communicating everything to everybody to whom I hadn't done so already, at once, now (2022/12/19), before I accept it.
